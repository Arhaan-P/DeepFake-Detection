## Overview

A gait-based deepfake detection system that analyzes walking patterns instead of
facial features. It extracts skeletal pose keypoints using MediaPipe and
evaluates them via a CNN+BiLSTM+Transformer hybrid model to determine if the
video is authentic or a deepfake.

## Data Flow

1. **Input:** Video and Claimed Identity are provided.
2. **Feature Extraction:** MediaPipe extracts 33 landmarks, converted to 78-dim
   gait features (coordinates, angles, velocities).
3. **Spatial Encoding:** 1D CNN GaitEncoder processes features.
4. **Temporal Modeling:** Dual-path BiLSTM + Transformer processes sequence
   dynamics.
5. **Verification:** Siamese IdentityVerifier compares the video embedding
   against enrolled identity signatures to predict Authentic or Deepfake.
